Amadeezy [International Chrome / Moveltraxx]
DJ - Travels from Boston, USA
Book
Omar Johan Cabrera is Amadeezy - a producer-DJ born and raised in Boston. Known as Beantown’s
Bass Boss, he was responsible for running the city's most prominent trap party, PVRPLE - credited for
bringing dirty south rap pioneers like DJ Paul of Three Six Mafia, Metroboomin and Manny Fresh to
the Massachusetts scene.
With a sound that reps all manner of electro, house, techno and hip hop - to G-styles like juke, jit and
ghettotech - standout records include the thugged out East Side G-Ride EP for Australian label
International Chrome - to a bevy of hyper hip hop influenced house records for Moveltraxx.
Racking up mad support from the likes of Mall Grab, Egyptian Lover and Cinthie to Ellen Alien and I
Hate Models - Amadeezy’s jams have made their way to Boys Noize Essential Mixes or DJ Stingray
Boiler Room sets - with featured air time on BBC Radio 1 and Rinse FM.
A specialist DJ with dope sets on Marseille radio Ola and a NTS session for Club Aerobics - pushing
Miami bass and Brazil funky - he also holds down his own monthly slot on Rinse FM France.
Furthermore, his Memphis-inspired electro mix Shake Junts made it to Mixmag’s ‘Best Mixes Of The
Year’ for 2020.
Taking his sound from the streets of Boston to Mardi Gras in New Orleans - Amadeezy has blazed a
trail far-and-wide with tours through India via New Delhi, Mumbai and Goa - to hitting up the
intersection of Europe with a peak-time set for the people of Barcelona.
Making his Berlin debut in 2021 alongside Jerome Hill, Projekt Gestalten and Young Lychee - the artist
is already floating a huge 2022 thanks to a raft of future vinyl releases already set for labels like
Partiboi69’s Mutual Pleasure, Evar Records, Fantastic Voyage and Nerang Recordings.
